Operational Feasibility

Staffing Requirements and Availability

Required Staff Positions

  1. Greenhouse Manager/Operator (1 position)
    • Responsibilities: Overall management of greenhouse operations, plant care, climate control, and maintenance scheduling
    • Skills required: Experience in greenhouse management, knowledge of ornamental plants, basic technical skills
    • Education: Agricultural or horticultural background preferred
    • Salary: SAR 2,000 per month (as specified in project parameters)
  2. Assistant/Laborer (1 position)
    • Responsibilities: Plant maintenance, watering, harvesting, packaging, and delivery assistance
    • Skills required: Basic plant care knowledge, physical capability for greenhouse work
    • Education: No specific requirements, on-the-job training
    • Salary: SAR 2,000 per month (as specified in project parameters)

Additional Staff Considerations

  • Part-time workers may be needed during peak seasons or for delivery/installation of plants to clients
  • Specialized consultants may be required periodically for technical issues or plant disease management
  • Expansion plan: Additional staff will be required if client base grows beyond 40 clients

Labor Market Assessment

  • Saudi Arabia has a mix of local and expatriate workers in the agricultural sector
  • Availability of skilled greenhouse workers may be limited in Al Kharj
  • Training programs may be necessary for local workers without greenhouse experience
  • Salary levels are competitive with agricultural sector averages in the region

Technical Expertise Requirements

Critical Technical Skills Needed

  1. Greenhouse Climate Management
    • Temperature and humidity control in extreme desert conditions
    • Operation of cooling and ventilation systems
    • Monitoring and adjustment of environmental parameters
  2. Irrigation and Water Management
    • Operation of drip irrigation systems
    • Water quality testing and treatment
    • Water conservation techniques
  3. Ornamental Plant Production
    • Propagation techniques for various ornamental species
    • Disease and pest identification and management
    • Growth scheduling and production planning
  4. Maintenance and Repair Skills
    • Basic electrical and plumbing repairs
    • Greenhouse structure maintenance
    • Equipment troubleshooting

Solutions for Technical Expertise Gaps

  • Initial consultation with greenhouse specialists during setup phase
  • Structured training program for staff on greenhouse operations
  • Ongoing relationship with agricultural extension services or consultants
  • Development of standard operating procedures (SOPs) for all technical processes
  • Subscription to online training resources and technical publications

  • Travel time: Approximately 1 hour to central Riyadh
  • Vehicle requirements: One small truck with plant transport capabilities
  • Service scheduling: Each client requires monthly maintenance visits

Risk Assessment and Mitigation

Operational Risks

  • Power outages: Backup generator system
  • Water supply interruption: Water storage tanks with 1-week capacity
  • Equipment failure: Preventive maintenance program, spare parts inventory
  • Pest/disease outbreak: Regular monitoring, integrated pest management
  • Staff turnover: Documented procedures, cross-training

Market Risks

  • Client loss: Diversified client base, excellent service quality
  • Price competition: Focus on quality and service differentiation
  • Market saturation: Expand service offerings, geographic expansion
  • Economic downturn: Flexible pricing strategies, cost control measures

Environmental Risks

  • Extreme heat events: Redundant cooling systems, heat-resistant plant selection
  • Water restrictions: Water-efficient systems, rainwater harvesting
  • Sandstorms: Robust greenhouse structure, proper sealing
  • Pest invasions: Physical barriers, monitoring systems

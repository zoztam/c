Executive Summary
Key findings and viability assessment

This feasibility study evaluates establishing a 400 SQM greenhouse in Al Kharj, Saudi Arabia, focused on ornamental plants production and maintenance services for Riyadh businesses.

Our analysis indicates the project is financially and operationally viable with a projected break-even period of 4.7 years and a 5-year ROI of 99.8%.

Market Analysis
Demand, competition, and opportunities

The Saudi Arabia Flower and Ornamental Plants Market is experiencing significant growth, with projections to reach several billion USD by 2033.

Riyadh represents a primary market for ornamental plants and related services, with high demand from commercial establishments, landscaping companies, and government projects.

Technical Requirements and Specifications

Climate Conditions in Al Kharj

Al Kharj presents challenging climate conditions for greenhouse operations:

  • Hot arid climate with temperatures ranging from 75-85°F (24-29°C)
  • Minimal annual precipitation (approximately 5 inches or less)
  • Increasing temperature trend due to climate change
  • Intense solar radiation
  • Water scarcity is a critical concern

These conditions necessitate specialized greenhouse design and technology to create suitable growing environments for ornamental plants.

Greenhouse Structure Requirements

Based on our research, the following greenhouse structures are recommended for the Al Kharj location:

  • Venlo-type greenhouse with good ventilation capabilities
  • High-quality polycarbonate or glass covering with UV protection
  • Robust structural design to withstand occasional high winds
  • East-West orientation to minimize direct sun exposure
  • Minimum height of 4 meters to allow for temperature stratification
  • Insulated north wall to reduce heat gain

Climate Control Systems

To maintain optimal growing conditions, the greenhouse will require:

  • Evaporative cooling system with fans and wet pads
  • Automated ventilation system with roof and side vents
  • Retractable internal shade screens (50-70% shade factor)
  • Potential for external shade nets during extreme summer conditions
  • Misting systems for humidity control
  • Air circulation fans for temperature uniformity

Irrigation and Water Management

Water efficiency is critical in Al Kharj's arid environment:

  • Automated drip irrigation system with individual plant control
  • Water recycling and collection system
  • Water quality monitoring and treatment equipment
  • Rainwater harvesting potential (limited but valuable)
  • Water storage capacity for minimum 7-day operation
  • Potential for integration with municipal water supply

Growing Systems and Equipment

The greenhouse will utilize:

  • Elevated growing benches for efficient space utilization
  • Specialized growing media suited to ornamental plants
  • Automated fertilizer injection system
  • Environmental monitoring sensors (temperature, humidity, light)
  • Plant propagation area for starting new plants
  • Specialized tools for plant maintenance and care

Energy Requirements

The greenhouse will require reliable energy sources to power climate control and irrigation systems:

  • Connection to municipal electricity grid
  • Backup generator system for power outages
  • Potential for solar panel integration to supplement power needs
  • Energy-efficient equipment and lighting
  • Automated control systems to optimize energy usage

Technology Integration

Modern technology will be integrated to enhance greenhouse operations:

  • Automated climate control systems with remote monitoring capabilities
  • IoT sensors for real-time environmental data collection
  • Computerized irrigation scheduling and monitoring
  • Data analytics for optimizing growing conditions
  • Mobile applications for remote monitoring and control
